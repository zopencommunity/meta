<h1 align="center">ZOPEN-AUDIT</h1>












<h2>NAME</h2>


<table><tr><td style="width: 25%; vertical-align: top;"><b>zopen-audit
- manual page for zopen-audit 0.8.5

<h2>SYNOPSIS</h2>



<b>zopen-audit</b>
[<i>OPTION</i>]

<h2>DESCRIPTION</h2>



zopen-audit
is a utility for zopen community to check for
vulnerabilities in your installed packages.

<h2>OPTIONS</h2>



<b>-v</b>,
<b>--verbose</b></td><td style="vertical-align: top;">run in verbose mode.</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 25%; vertical-align: top;"><b>--version</b></td><td style="vertical-align: top;">print version.</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 25%; vertical-align: top;"><b>-u</b>,
<b>--update</b>,
<b>--upgrade</b></td><td style="vertical-align: top;">attempt to resolve
vulnerabilities by upgrading packages.</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 25%; vertical-align: top;"><b>-r</b>,
<b>--remove</b></td><td style="vertical-align: top;">remove packages with
vulnerabilities.</td></tr></table>

<h2>EXAMPLES</h2>


<table><tr><td style="width: 25%; vertical-align: top;"><b>zopen audit</b></td><td style="vertical-align: top;">check for vulnerabilities in
all installed packages</td></tr></table>
