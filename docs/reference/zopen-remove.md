<h1 align="center">ZOPEN-REMOVE</h1>




<h2>NAME</h2>


<p style="margin-left:6%; margin-top: 1em">zopen-remove -
manual page for zopen-remove 0.8.5</p>

<h2>SYNOPSIS</h2>



<p style="margin-left:6%; margin-top: 1em">zopen-remove
[OPTION] [PACKAGE] ...</p>

<h2>DESCRIPTION</h2>


<p style="margin-left:6%; margin-top: 1em">zopen-remove is
a utility for zopen community to remove an installed package
or packages.</p>

<h2>OPTIONS</h2>


<p style="margin-left:6%; margin-top: 1em">-h,
--help, -?</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">display this help and exit.</p>

<p style="margin-left:6%;">-p, --purge</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">remove package, the versioned
directory, and any cached files.</p>

<p style="margin-left:6%;">-v, --verbose</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">run in verbose mode.</p>

<p style="margin-left:6%;">--version</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">print version.</p>

<p style="margin-left:6%;">-y, --yes</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">automatically answer yes to
prompts.</p>

<h2>EXAMPLES</h2>


<p style="margin-left:6%; margin-top: 1em">zopen remove foo
bar</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">interactively remove the foo
and bar packages if installed</p>

<p style="margin-left:6%;">zopen remove --yes foo bar</p>

<p style="margin-left:15%;">remove the foo and bar packages
if installed, without asking for confirmation</p>
